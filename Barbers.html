meet the

BARBERS

ben — hails from Middlesex County and has had a passion for cutting hair since he was a kid. He honed his craft back in high school where he kept an ever-increasing number of classmates looking sharp. His signature cut is a classic combover with detailed scissor work. Spends free time with family and on the golf course.

Youssef — a third-generation barber who started cutting hair in Morocco when he was just 13. Very laid-back personality, but holds himself to exacting standards when he’s cutting. Loves to reinterpret and modernize classic cuts that he free-hands to shape the face of each client. He will talk European soccer for hours.

Armando – moved to the US from Colombia when he was 12. Quiet and focused, he prefers to let his work do the talking. Takes great pleasure in making clients happy and sharpening their look. A certified car-lover, he appreciates both classic models as well as modern sports

Shane — reared in Middlesex County and worked as a Master Barber instructor at the New England Hair Academy before joining the team at Tweed. His specialties are fades and precise scissor cuts. He’s a devoted follower of all Boston sports teams, so for best results don’t wear a Yankees’ cap when you come to see him.

George — a native of Armenia, with over 20 years of barbering experience. Loves high-end sports cars almost as much as life itself. Proud father of two beautiful kids. Often mistaken for Adrian Brody.

Charlie — Hair runs in the family, as his mother and two older sisters also work in the industry. Believes that the key to building the most effective client relationship is building comfort and trust. Loves the Red Sox, and had a wicked fastball that topped out at 90 mph in high school.

Midou — came to Tweed Barbers from Paris, where he cut hair for almost 15 years. A gentle soul who carefully crafts his haircuts to match the face shape of his clients. When he’s not working, you’ll find him spending time with his wife and baby daughter or watching soccer on television.

Ali — a second-generation barber who learned the craft from his father and has been cutting hair for over 35 years. A very fluid set of hands and a true master at razor-textured cuts. He speaks four languages and loves to cook exotic Moroccan dishes that he generously shares with his co-workers.

shon — very meticulous and studious in his approach. He loves to talk about vintage cars and is a highly talented graphic artist in his free time. His signature cut is the 1950s pompadour, which is a very labor-intensive cut and currently back in vogue.
